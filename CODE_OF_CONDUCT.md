# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Ootils is a technical project. We hold one standard: **be direct, be honest, be constructive.**

We welcome:
- Technical disagreement backed by argument
- War stories from real supply chain failures
- Hard questions about architectural decisions
- "This won't work because..." — with a reason

We do not tolerate:
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Marketing speak without technical substance
- "Why don't you just use [existing tool]?" without genuine architectural argument
- Dismissiveness toward practitioners who don't have a CS background
- Dismissiveness toward engineers who don't have SC operations experience

Both worlds — supply chain and engineering — are needed here. Respect that.

## Enforcement

Violations will result in removal from the community. No warnings for harassment. One warning for persistent low-quality contributions.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and any community channels.

## Contact

Open a private discussion or email the maintainer directly via GitHub.
